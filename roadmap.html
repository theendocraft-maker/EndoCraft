Session Scroll

Moments → Sealed forever.

Paste your session recap. AI finds the moment worth immortalizing. A D20 rolls server-side. Cinematic art generates. Your card is sealed — Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, or Misprint. Three cards free, no signup.

"The party went silent when I showed them the card."

DM Cockpit + Player Companion

The full table in one app.

DMs get the Cockpit: campaign tree, AI Co-DM, live combat tracker, encounter builder, read-aloud generator. Players get the Companion: character sheet, live session updates, 3D dice, party album. One magic link from the DM and everyone's in.

"I ran my best session ever. And I have the cards to prove it."

Animated card art — subtle motion, glowing effects, weather and atmosphere. Your Legendary card doesn't just sit there. It moves. Available to Legend-tier subscribers and Patreon supporters in beta.

Automatically compiled from your session recaps and location notes. Every NPC, faction, place, and plot thread — searchable, linked, always up to date. Ask your AI Co-DM "what do we know about House Velrath?" and it tells you everything your party's uncovered.
