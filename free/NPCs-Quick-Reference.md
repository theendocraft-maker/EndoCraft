# 5 D&D NPC Cards · Quick Reference

*Drop any of these into tonight's session.*

---

## 01 · Brennan the Innkeeper

**Race:** Dwarf · **Age:** 60s · **Vibe:** Warm, watchful

**Three Traits:**
1. Knows everyone in the village by name — and their secrets
2. Polishes the same mug for an hour while listening
3. Keeps a small dagger under the bar "for keeping the peace"

**Plot Hook:**
> *Brennan slides over a sealed letter and whispers: "She left this for whoever asked about her. You're the only one who has."*

---

## 02 · Vex the Wandering Bard

**Race:** Half-Elf · **Age:** Looks 20s, claims to remember things from 200 years ago · **Vibe:** Mysterious, melancholy

**Three Traits:**
1. Never says her real name — Vex is the third one this week
2. Plays the same sad melody at every tavern she enters
3. Watches the party without ever quite looking at them

**Plot Hook:**
> *After playing a song that makes one party member cry, Vex sits down and says: "That song was about you. I don't know how I know it yet."*

---

## 03 · Ser Cadwell the Knight

**Race:** Human · **Age:** 50s · **Vibe:** Stoic, principled, exhausted

**Three Traits:**
1. Swore an oath to a kingdom that no longer exists
2. Still wears the heraldry of his fallen lord
3. Never raises his voice. Never repeats himself.

**Plot Hook:**
> *Ser Cadwell offers to escort the party for free. "I have nothing else to do, and you look like you'll need a sword arm before the week ends."*

---

## 04 · Mira the Hedge-Witch

**Race:** Human · **Age:** 70s · **Vibe:** Knowing, prickly, secretly kind

**Three Traits:**
1. The villagers fear her — and visit her at midnight when they need help
2. Reads tea leaves, palms, and people equally well
3. Owes a debt to something old in the forest

**Plot Hook:**
> *Mira hands the party a small wooden charm: "Don't open this unless the moon is wrong. You'll know which one I mean."*

---

## 05 · Thalon the Mercenary Captain

**Race:** Half-Orc · **Age:** 30s · **Vibe:** Hard-edged, careful, surprisingly literate

**Three Traits:**
1. Collects old maps — particularly maps of places that don't exist anymore
2. Trusts the second instinct, not the first
3. Has a reputation for never breaking a contract — until he does

**Plot Hook:**
> *Thalon spreads an old map and points to a blank spot: "I've been here. The map doesn't show it. I'd like to know why before I go back."*

---

## How to Use These NPCs

Each NPC is **system-agnostic** — no stat blocks lock you in. Use the traits and hooks as inspiration. Statblock them yourself for D&D 5e (Veteran for Cadwell, Bandit Captain for Thalon, Spy for Vex, Acolyte for Mira, Commoner for Brennan).

**Hand the cards to your players** — the portraits make NPCs memorable. People remember faces, not names.
