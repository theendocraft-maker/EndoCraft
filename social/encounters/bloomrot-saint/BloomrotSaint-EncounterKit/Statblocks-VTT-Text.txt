THE BLOOMROT SAINT
Large celestial, neutral  ·  CR 11 (7,200 XP)
Armor Class 17 (blessed plate)
Hit Points 168 (16d10 + 80)
Speed 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)
STR 18 (+4)  DEX 12 (+1)  CON 18 (+4)  INT 10 (+0)  WIS 15 (+2)  CHA 16 (+3)
Saving Throws CON +8, WIS +6, CHA +7
Skills Insight +6, Perception +6
Damage Resistances radiant; bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks
Condition Immunities charmed, exhaustion, frightened
Senses truesight 30 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 16
Languages Celestial, Common; telepathy 60 ft.
Proficiency Bonus +4

TRAITS
Magic Resistance. Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
Radiant Petals. When the Saint takes damage, luminous petals burst. Each creature within 5 ft. must make a DC 16 Constitution save or gain 1 Radiance.
Sorrow's Grace. Advantage on saves against being knocked prone or moved against her will.
Second Bloom. The first time the Saint drops to 0 HP, she instead drops to 60 HP and enters Full Bloom (Phase 2): lair actions trigger on initiative 20 and 10; Petalfall recharges on 4-6 and widens to 30 ft.; when the Saint uses Renewal, creatures within 10 ft. gain 1 Radiance.
Radiance (Condition). Radiance is a stacking blessing that burns. At 3 Radiance a creature is Overbright: disadvantage on attack rolls and cannot regain HP until all Radiance fades. Lesser restoration removes 1 Radiance; a short rest removes 1.

ACTIONS
Multiattack. The Saint makes two Notched Blade attacks.
Notched Blade. Melee Weapon Attack (magical): +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d8 + 4) slashing plus 9 (2d8) radiant, and the target gains 1 Radiance.
Petalfall (Recharge 5-6). 15-ft cone of shining petals. DC 16 Dexterity save: 27 (6d8) radiant on a fail (half on success); creatures that fail gain 1 Radiance.

LEGENDARY ACTIONS (3/round)
Drift. Fly up to half speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Radiant Lash. One creature within 30 ft. makes a DC 16 CON save or gains 1 Radiance.
Renewal (Costs 2). The Saint regains 15 HP if below maximum.

LAIR ACTIONS (initiative 20; also 10 in Full Bloom)
- Blossoming vines erupt in a 10-ft radius: difficult terrain; DC 14 STR save or restrained (escape DC 14).
- A cloud of glowing motes (20-ft sphere); a creature entering/starting its turn there gains 1 Radiance.
