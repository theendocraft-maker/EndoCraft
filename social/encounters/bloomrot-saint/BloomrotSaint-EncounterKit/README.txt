WHO SHE IS
A fallen celestial — the last mourning angel of a god that went silent. Her grief
overflowed into an endless, radiant bloom. Beautiful, tragic, and deadly.

TWO WAYS TO PLAY (one kit)
- BOSS: a two-phase legendary finale at a drowned altar.
- TRASH MOB: drop 3–6 Bloomrot Husks into any ruin, chapel or crypt.

THE RADIANCE MECHANIC
Radiance is a stacking blessing that burns. At 3 Radiance a creature is Overbright
(disadvantage on attacks, no healing) until cleansed. Call the count aloud — the dread is the point.
