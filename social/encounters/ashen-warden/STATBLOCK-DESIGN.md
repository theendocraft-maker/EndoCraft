# THE ASHEN WARDEN — Ashen Realm Encounter (5e Homebrew)

*A fallen guardian who still keeps his post, long after the kingdom that set him there turned to ash. He guards nothing now. He will not yield.*
**CR 5 · 100% original homebrew · single-boss (or 1+2 minions) · for a 4-person party of ~4th–6th level.**
**ROLE: the free lead-magnet — a taste of the Ashen Realm series.**

---

## SIGNATURE MECHANIC — EMBER-GUARD (the parry)

The Warden fights from a raised guard. **The first time each round it is hit by a melee attack, it may use its reaction to parry:** reduce that attack's damage by **1d10 + 3**. If the damage is reduced to 0, the Warden immediately **ripostes** with one *Ashen Blade* attack against the attacker.

**The tell / the lesson:** bait the guard — throw a small or ranged hit to burn its reaction — *then* land the real blow while its parry is spent. A party that just swings gets riposted; a party that learns the rhythm breaks him. That's Soulslike in one line of rules.

## STATBLOCK

**The Ashen Warden**
*Medium undead (hollow), neutral*

- **Armor Class** 18 (battered plate, raised guard)
- **Hit Points** 97 (13d8 + 39)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 16 (+3) | 11 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 8 (−1) | 12 (+1) | 9 (−1) |

- **Saving Throws** STR +6, CON +6
- **Damage Immunities** poison; **Condition Immunities** exhaustion, frightened, poisoned
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
- **Languages** understands the tongue it knew in life but cannot speak
- **Challenge** 5 (1,800 XP) · **Proficiency Bonus** +3

**Ashen Weapons.** The Warden's weapon attacks are magical and deal an extra 1d8 necrotic damage (included below).

**Undying Post.** The first time the Warden drops to 0 hit points, it instead drops to 1 hit point, its ember-eyes flaring as it plants its blade and refuses to fall (once per encounter).

### ACTIONS
**Multiattack.** The Warden makes two *Ashen Blade* attacks.

**Ashen Blade.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing plus 4 (1d8) necrotic damage.

**Cinder Sweep (Recharge 5–6).** The Warden sweeps its blade in a 15-ft. cone of embers. Each creature in that area makes a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) fire damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

### REACTION
**Ember-Guard.** *See signature mechanic above.* The first melee hit against the Warden each round: parry (reduce damage by 1d10 + 3); if reduced to 0, riposte with one *Ashen Blade*.

---

### RUNNING IT
- **Scaling:** solo it's a tense duel for a 4th-level party; add 2 *ashen husk* minions (use **commoner** with 10 HP and a shortsword) for 6th level.
- **The teaching moment:** early in the fight a martial PC swings, gets parried and riposted — now the table *gets it*, and starts baiting the guard. That "aha" is the whole point.
- **Emotional beat:** if the party lets him live at 1 HP (Undying Post), he simply returns to his post and bows once. He was never the villain — only the last soldier of a dead order.

### READ-ALOUD
> *The gate has no wall left to guard, no king left to serve. But the knight is still here, blade planted, embers dim behind a cracked visor. He does not challenge you. He simply raises his guard — and waits for you to learn.*
