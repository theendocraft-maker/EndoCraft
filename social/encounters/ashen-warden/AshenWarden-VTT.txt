THE ASHEN WARDEN
Medium undead (hollow), neutral
Armor Class 18 (battered plate, raised guard)
Hit Points 97 (13d8 + 39)
Speed 30 ft.

STR 16 (+3)  DEX 11 (+0)  CON 16 (+3)  INT 8 (-1)  WIS 12 (+1)  CHA 9 (-1)

Saving Throws STR +6, CON +6
Damage Immunities poison
Condition Immunities exhaustion, frightened, poisoned
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
Languages understands the tongue it knew in life but cannot speak
Challenge 5 (1,800 XP)   Proficiency Bonus +3

TRAITS
Ashen Weapons. The Warden's weapon attacks are magical and deal an extra 1d8 necrotic damage (included below).
Undying Post (1/encounter). The first time the Warden drops to 0 hit points, it instead drops to 1 hit point.

ACTIONS
Multiattack. The Warden makes two Ashen Blade attacks.
Ashen Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing plus 4 (1d8) necrotic damage.
Cinder Sweep (Recharge 5-6). The Warden sweeps its blade in a 15-foot cone of embers. Each creature in that area makes a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) fire damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

REACTION
Ember-Guard. The first time each round the Warden is hit by a melee attack, it reduces that attack's damage by 1d10 + 3. If the damage is reduced to 0, the Warden immediately makes one Ashen Blade attack against the attacker.

RUNNING IT
- Bait the guard: a small or ranged hit burns his reaction, then land the real blow.
- Scaling: solo for a 4th-level party; add two ashen husks (commoner, 10 HP, shortsword) for 6th level.
- If the party leaves him standing at 1 HP, he returns to his post and bows once.
