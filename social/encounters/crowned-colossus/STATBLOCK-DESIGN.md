# THE CROWNED COLOSSUS — Ashen Realm Flagship Encounter (5e Homebrew)

*A dead god that never learned it died. Its body is the arena; its grief is the fight.*
**CR 16 · 100% original homebrew · 3 phases · for a 4–5 person party of ~13th–15th level.**

---

## THE SIGNATURE MECHANIC — GRAVE-WEIGHT

The Colossus does not burn you. It **buries** you under the weight of a dead god's sorrow.

- **Grave-Weight** is a stacking condition (0–5). Many of the Colossus's attacks and the arena itself apply 1 stack.
- **Each stack:** your speed is reduced by 5 ft, and you take a **−1 penalty to attack rolls and saving throws** (cumulative).
- **At 5 stacks (Interred):** you fall prone and are **restrained** as the weight drives you to your knees. You can spend your whole turn (an action) to claw off 1 stack; allies adjacent to you can use an action to remove 1 stack from you.
- **Shedding weight:** at the end of your turn, if you moved at least 20 ft this turn, remove 1 stack. (Soulslike logic: keep moving, or be crushed.) A short rest removes all stacks.

Grave-Weight is the fight's clock. The party must manage it while learning the boss's tells — stand still and you die; keep moving and you have a chance.

## STATBLOCK

**The Crowned Colossus**
*Gargantuan celestial (fallen god), neutral*

- **Armor Class** 19 (ancient blessed stone)
- **Hit Points** 350 (20d20 + 140) — *see Phases*
- **Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 27 (+8) | 8 (−1) | 24 (+7) | 6 (−2) | 16 (+3) | 22 (+6) |

- **Saving Throws** CON +13, WIS +9, CHA +12
- **Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
- **Damage Immunities** poison, psychic; **Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, prone (Phases 1–2), stunned
- **Senses** truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 13
- **Languages** understands Celestial but cannot speak
- **Challenge** 16 (15,000 XP) · **Proficiency Bonus** +5

**Weight of a Dead God.** Any creature that starts its turn within 15 ft. of the Colossus gains 1 Grave-Weight (no save). While the Colossus is Interred (Phase 3 body), this aura ends.

**Failing Divinity.** The Colossus is immune to the effects of its own Grave-Weight. When it is reduced to 0 hit points for the first time, it does not die — it enters **The Weeping Heart** (Phase 3).

**Magic Weapons.** The Colossus's attacks are magical.

### PHASES
- **Phase 1 — The Slumbering God (350 → 234 HP).** Ponderous. Uses *Slam* and *Sunder*. Grave-Weight aura is 20 ft. It has not yet fully woken.
- **Phase 2 — The Waking Wrath (234 → 0 HP).** At 234 HP it rises: its crown ignites and molten cracks open. It gains the *Crown of Ash* action and a second legendary action each round. Speed becomes 50 ft.
- **Phase 3 — The Weeping Heart.** When the body drops to 0, it kneels — **Interred** and inert — and its chest splits, exposing the **Heart-Core** (AC 17, 90 HP, immune to Grave-Weight aura, no legendary actions). Destroy the Heart to end the god. While the Heart lives, the fallen body still takes *Lair Actions*.

---

### ACTIONS

**Multiattack.** The Colossus makes two attacks: one *Slam* and one *Sunder* (Phase 2+: it may replace one with *Crown of Ash* when available).

**Slam.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. *Hit:* 27 (4d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage, and the target gains 1 Grave-Weight.

**Sunder.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. *Hit:* 22 (3d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage. The target must succeed on a DC 19 Strength save or be knocked prone and gain 1 Grave-Weight.

**Crown of Ash (Phase 2+, Recharge 5–6).** The crown flares. Each creature in a 40-ft. cone makes a DC 18 Dexterity save, taking 45 (10d8) radiant damage and gaining 2 Grave-Weight on a failure, or half damage and 1 Grave-Weight on a success.

### LEGENDARY ACTIONS
The Colossus can take 3 legendary actions (Phase 1: only 2), choosing from below. Only one option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Spent actions refresh at the start of its turn.

- **Step (Costs 1).** The Colossus moves up to half its speed. Each creature whose space it passes through gains 1 Grave-Weight.
- **Mourner's Toll (Costs 1).** One creature within 60 ft. must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution save or gain 1 Grave-Weight as the god's sorrow presses down.
- **Ruin (Costs 2).** The Colossus slams the ground. Each creature within 20 ft. of a point it can see makes a DC 18 Dexterity save, taking 21 (6d6) bludgeoning damage and falling prone on a failure.

### LAIR ACTIONS (the collapsing divine body)
On initiative count 20 (losing ties), the arena acts:
- **Falling Reliquary.** Rubble crashes down in a 10-ft.-radius area; a creature there makes a DC 17 Dexterity save or takes 16 (3d10) bludgeoning damage and gains 1 Grave-Weight.
- **Sea of Ash.** Ash floods a 20-ft.-radius zone (difficult terrain, lightly obscured) until initiative 20 next round; a creature that ends its turn there gains 1 Grave-Weight.
- **The God Remembers.** One creature that dealt the most damage last round hears a dead god's grief; it must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom save or gain 1 Grave-Weight.

---

### RUNNING THE FIGHT (DM notes)
- **The tell:** the crown brightens the round before *Crown of Ash* — telegraph it, reward players who spread out and keep moving.
- **The turn:** Grave-Weight punishes standing still; the arena is huge on purpose (fight *around* the god). Melee characters need an exit plan.
- **The heart:** Phase 3 is a sprint — the body kneels but the arena keeps killing (lair actions), so it's a race to the exposed Heart. Emotional beat: the god finally stops fighting and simply grieves as it dies.

### READ-ALOUD (the reveal)
> *The crown has not gone out in a thousand years. Beneath it, the stone face does not know you are there — does not know the war it lost, the god it was, the death it never noticed. It only knows the weight. And now, so will you.*
