Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

EndoCraft (“we”, “us”) creates and sells digital Dungeons & Dragons art assets at endocraft.app and on Etsy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

If you sign up for our free pack, we collect your email address to send the download and occasional updates. We use privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible) that do not use cookies or track individuals.

TikTok integration

We use the official TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload our own marketing videos as drafts to our own EndoCraft TikTok account. We do not access, store, or share any other TikTok user data.

How we use data

To deliver products you request, send updates you opted into, and improve our shop. We never sell your data.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time. To request deletion of your data, contact marco@endocraft.app.
