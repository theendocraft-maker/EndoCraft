CINEMATIC BOSS REVEALS
Six table-ready monster cinematics · by EndoCraft
================================================================

WHAT'S INSIDE
-------------
Six short cinematic "boss reveal" clips, hand-picked from a lot of
rejected ones. Drop one on screen the moment your party meets the
thing — then cut your voice and let the room go quiet.

  01  The Hollow King      — a crowned dead monarch on his throne
  02  The Iron Warden      — the guardian set here to stop them
  03  The Crowned Colossus — when something enormous stands up
  04  The Lich             — the archmage who cheated death
  05  The Eye Tyrant       — paranoia given a single, vast eye
  06  The Mind Eater       — the far-realm horror that wants your skull

FORMAT
------
- Vertical 9:16, 1080 x 1920, full HD
- ~10 seconds each, MP4 (H.264)
- SILENT on purpose — no baked-in music, so you run them under your
  own table ambience or stream audio without two tracks fighting.

HOW TO PLAY THEM AT THE TABLE
-----------------------------
- Prop a phone or tablet upright where the party can see it, hit play
  on the reveal, and start describing. The vertical frame fills a
  phone/tablet edge to edge.
- Streaming or on Discord? Drop the MP4 straight in — it's already the
  right shape for a phone-first audience, a stinger, or a scene card.
- Loop it: each clip is built to sit on screen for a beat. Play once
  for the reveal, or let it ride while the fight starts.
- No dialogue, no captions, no watermark. Your narration stays in charge.

WHEN TO PLAY EACH
-----------------
01  THE HOLLOW KING
    The party breaches the throne room and he's still sitting there.
    Undead royalty, an oathbreaker, a king who refused to die.

02  THE IRON WARDEN
    A sealed door, a vault, a bridge — and the thing built to hold it.
    Animated armor, a construct sentinel, the guardian that won't move
    until they make it.

03  THE CROWNED COLOSSUS
    The moment something the size of a building stands up. A titan, an
    awakened statue, the big set-piece the whole session was building to.

04  THE LICH
    The reveal of the campaign's undead mastermind — the one who's been
    three steps ahead the entire time. Phylactery optional. Menace not.

05  THE EYE TYRANT
    When the aberration in the dark finally opens its eye and every
    hallway suddenly feels watched. A floating horror, a dungeon's
    paranoid heart.

06  THE MIND EATER
    The far-realm thing behind the cult, the puppet-master pulling the
    strings from somewhere your maps don't reach. Play it when the
    party realizes what they've actually been fighting.
