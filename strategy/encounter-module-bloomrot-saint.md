# Encounter-Modul (Pilot) — „The Bloomrot Saint"

**Konzept-Prinzip:** soulslike-Style als Inspiration, Inhalt 100 % eigen → kein IP-Risiko, voller Statblock erlaubt.
**Dual-Use (USP):** dieselbe Kreatur als **epischer Legendär-Boss** ODER als **Trashmob** (mehrere „Husks" für ein x-beliebiges Abenteuer). Ein Asset-Kit, zwei Spielweisen.

---

## 1) Kreatur-Konzept
Einst eine heilige Kriegerin, deren Wunden nie heilten — bis ein schöner, leuchtender **Blüten-Rot** sie übernahm. Sie bewegt sich mit unheimlicher Anmut; aus den Spalten ihrer goldenen Rüstung wachsen blasse Blüten, und ihre Sporen lassen alles um sie langsam verrotten. Tragisch, schön, bedrohlich.

## 3) Boss-Statblock — *The Bloomrot Saint* (5e Homebrew)
*Large blighted undead, neutral evil · CR 11 (7,200 XP)*
- **AC** 17 (blighted plate) · **HP** 168 (16d10+80) · **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 18 (+4) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 18 (+4) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 15 (+2) **CHA** 16 (+3)
- **Saves** CON +8, WIS +6 · **Skills** Perception +6
- **Resistances** necrotic · **Immunities** poison; poisoned, frightened
- **Senses** blindsight 30 ft, passive Perception 16 · **Languages** understands Common
- **Proficiency** +4

**Traits**
- *Blooming Rot.* When the Saint takes damage, spores burst: each creature within 5 ft must make a DC 16 CON save or gain **1 Rot** (see below).
- *Saint's Grace.* Advantage on saves vs. being knocked prone or moved against its will.
- *Second Bloom.* The first time it drops to 0 HP, it instead drops to 60 HP and enters **Full Bloom** (lair erupts; Petalfall recharges on 4–6).

**Rot (condition).** Each Rot is a stacking blight. At **3 Rot**, a creature is **Blighted**: disadvantage on attack rolls and can't regain HP until it removes all Rot (a *lesser restoration* removes 1; a short rest removes 1).

**Actions**
- *Multiattack.* Two Notched Blade attacks.
- *Notched Blade.* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d8+4) slashing + 9 (2d8) necrotic, and the target gains 1 Rot.
- *Petalfall (Recharge 5–6).* 15-ft cone of razor petals. DC 16 DEX save: 27 (6d8) slashing on a fail (half on success), and creatures that fail gain 1 Rot.

**Legendary Actions (3/round, regain at start of turn)**
- *Drift.* Move up to half speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- *Spore Lash.* One creature within 30 ft makes a DC 16 CON save or gains 1 Rot.
- *Bloom (costs 2).* The Saint regains 15 HP if below max.

**Lair Actions (initiative 20)**
- Roots erupt in a 10-ft radius: difficult terrain, and a creature there makes a DC 14 STR save or is restrained until it breaks free (DC 14).
- A drifting spore cloud (20-ft sphere) lightly obscures the area; a creature entering or starting its turn there gains 1 Rot.

## 4) Trashmob-Statblock — *Bloomrot Husk* (dasselbe Asset, runtergebrochen)
*Medium undead · CR 1/2 (100 XP) · field 3–6 as a regular encounter*
- **AC** 12 · **HP** 26 (4d8+8) · **Speed** 20 ft
- **STR** 14 (+2) **DEX** 10 (+0) **CON** 14 (+2) **WIS** 8 (−1)
- **Resistances** necrotic · **Immunities** poisoned
- *Burst on Death.* When it dies, spores burst (5 ft, DC 12 CON or 1 Rot).
- **Slam.** +4 to hit, *Hit:* 7 (1d8+2) bludgeoning; on a roll of 5+ on the d8, the target also gains 1 Rot.

→ Same art/token; DMs drop a few Husks into any dungeon for flavor, or face the Saint as a finale.

## 5) Encounter-Hooks (beide Einsätze)
- **Epic:** the party tracks a spreading blight to a drowned chapel — the Saint waits at the altar, blooming.
- **Trashmob:** Husks shamble through any ruin/swamp/crypt; a quick, atmospheric fight that seeds the bigger threat.
