SESSION SUMMARY

The party descended into the crypt chasing rumors of the Ashborne relic. Halls were narrow, lit only by torchlight, and Lyra's ranger senses flagged something massive moving ahead.

They found it in the final chamber — a dread hound three times the size of any dog, eyes burning. Mara's spells were spent; Aldric's shield was split. Thorne stepped forward alone and drove his blade through the beast's heart on a clean nat 20. The party carried the relic out in silence.

NPC LOG

Keeper Veylen — Old, stooped, blind in one eye. Gave the party the crypt map in exchange for a promise to return it. Seemed afraid of what they'd find.

The Dread Hound (deceased) — Unknown origin. Three times dog-size. Burned eyes. Guarded the Ashborne relic. Slain by Thorne in session 14.
