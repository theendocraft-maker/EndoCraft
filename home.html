✦ FAQ

Still have questions?

What exactly is EndoCraft — and who is it for?
▾
EndoCraft is a D&D session platform for the whole table. DMs get a full campaign cockpit: prep tools, an AI Co-DM, live combat tracker, and automated session cards. Players get a companion app with their character sheet, live updates, and a shared party album. It works entirely in the browser — no install needed. Designed for 5e but adaptable to any TTRPG.
Do I need to install anything? Does my group need to sign up?
▾
Nothing to install — EndoCraft runs entirely in your browser. The Session Scroll tool doesn't require any account at all: paste your recap, get your card. For the full Cockpit, DMs create a free account. Players join via a magic link the DM sends — no signup, no password. One tap and they're in during the session.
How does rarity work — can I buy a Legendary card?
▾
No. Rarity is determined by a D20 roll on our server the moment you submit — Common (2–6), Rare (7–13), Epic (14–19), Legendary (Nat 20, 8%), and Misprint (Nat 1, 3%). You can re-roll a moment for €0.50 with a different art style, but the rarity is always re-rolled. No amount of money guarantees you a Legendary. That's the point.
When are Living Cards and Campaign Memory coming?
▾
Both are in active development. Living Cards (animated, glowing card art) and Campaign Memory (recurring NPCs, plot threads across sessions) are the next major features. Check the Roadmap for current status, or join the waitlist below to be notified the moment they drop. Patreon supporters get beta access first.
